## Use "Mermaid" For Flow Diagrams  

It is just a fantastic toolkit.  

Here is a Mermaid block:  

```mermaid 
graph TD;
  Root-->Node1;
  Node1-->Node3;
  Node1-->Node2;
  Root-->Node2;
  Node2-->Node3;
```
  
  This is below the Mermaid block.
